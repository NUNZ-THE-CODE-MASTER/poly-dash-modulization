About Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based platforming game that requires players to navigate through various levels by jumping over obstacles and collecting stars.

Get ready to experience a fun and challenging adventure!

Our Story

Geometry Dash was launched in 2013 by a passionate developer named Aba Nuni. What began as a simple idea for a rhythm-based platformer quickly transformed into a global phenomenon. With its engaging gameplay, custom level editor, and an enthusiastic community, Geometry Dash has captivated players around the world. Our team continues to work tirelessly to bring exciting updates and fresh content to keep the gaming experience vibrant and enjoyable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver an exhilarating gaming experience that challenges players' reflexes and creativity. We believe in the power of community and strive to foster an environment where players can share their creativity through user-generated levels. Whether you’re a newcomer or a seasoned player, we aim to provide a platform that encourages fun, challenge, and artistic expression in the world of gaming.
